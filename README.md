# Michigan MindMend Inc. — Advanced AI Research Lab

**Privacy-first, offline AI systems engineered for trust-critical environments. Advancing the frontier of secure, performant, and compassionate artificial intelligence.**

Welcome to **Michigan MindMend Inc.**, a cutting-edge AI research and development organization dedicated to building production-grade AI systems that prioritize privacy, security, and human wellbeing. Our work spans high-performance GPU kernels, advanced cognitive architectures, enterprise security layers, and youth mental health applications—all grounded in the principle that AI should serve people, not extract value from them.

## 🚀 Core Research & Engineering Areas

### High-Performance AI Infrastructure

We push the boundaries of GPU computing and LLM inference efficiency through specialized kernel development and architectural optimization.

- **[DominusUltra](https://github.com/MiMindMendinc/DominusUltra)**: State-of-the-art Triton CUDA kernel for fused RoPE causal attention with GQA and FP8 support. Achieves **25M+ tokens/sec** on NVIDIA T4 and **185M+ tokens/sec** on A100 GPUs. Engineered for production LLM inference on edge hardware.

- **[waitlist-pow-solver](https://github.com/MiMindMendinc/waitlist-pow-solver)**: High-speed Proof-of-Work solver for Cloudflare Worker waitlists, demonstrating expertise in cryptographic algorithms and browser-based optimization.

### Privacy-First Security & Compliance

Our security layer architecture ensures LLM applications meet enterprise compliance requirements while maintaining zero data egress.

- **[-privguard](https://github.com/MiMindMendinc/-privguard)**: Production-grade privacy and security gateway for LLM applications. Features automated PII redaction (50+ entity types), secret scrubbing, prompt injection blocking, and immutable decision logging. Designed for HIPAA, GDPR, and CCPA compliance.

- **[TrustLayer](https://github.com/MiMindMendinc/TrustLayer)**: Lightweight Node.js API proxy for LLM safety and compliance. Provides request/response interception, jailbreak detection, topic blocking, and deterministic audit logging. Optimized for minimal latency in production environments.

### Advanced Cognitive Architectures

We develop sophisticated multi-agent AI systems capable of continuous learning, self-improvement, and autonomous reasoning.

- **[Cognitive-Memory-V4](https://github.com/MiMindMendinc/Cognitive-Memory-V4)**: A self-improving multi-agent cognitive architecture featuring long-term memory (Qdrant-backed), dream-based memory consolidation, reinforcement learning, contradiction detection, and autonomous reasoning. Represents the frontier of AI system design.

- **[OpenClaw-Empathy-Anchor](https://github.com/MiMindMendinc/OpenClaw-Empathy-Anchor-MindMend-OpenClaw-)**: Customized OpenClaw.ai integration with empathy anchoring for ethical, offline AI supporting youth mental health and safety in Michigan.

### Youth Safety & Mental Health AI

Specialized AI systems designed to protect vulnerable populations while maintaining privacy and building trust.

- **[mindmend-guardian](https://github.com/MiMindMendinc/mindmend-guardian)**: Full youth safety AI featuring gentle voice interaction, child protection backend, and <1W always-on capability. Designed for continuous protection with zero cloud dependency.

- **[mindmend-vault-private](https://github.com/MiMindMendinc/mindmend-vault-private)**: Offline crisis AI that activates when internet connectivity is lost, ensuring youth have access to support even in network outages. Watermarked, zero-log architecture.

### Tactical Privacy Tools

Specialized tools for journalists, field teams, and NGOs operating in high-risk environments.

- **[Tactical-FieldKit](https://github.com/MiMindMendinc/Tactical-FieldKit)**: 100% offline SITREP generator and image sanitizer. Zero network, zero metadata leaks. Built for field teams, law enforcement, journalists, and NGOs.

- **[Tactical-BurnerContacts](https://github.com/MiMindMendinc/Tactical-BurnerContacts)**: Offline burner contact manager PWA with auto-wipe after 30 days and panic-wipe via triple-shake. Companion to Tactical-FieldKit.

### Productivity & Intelligence

Practical AI tools that enhance human capability while respecting privacy.

- **[SnapNote](https://github.com/MiMindMendinc/SnapNote)**: Turn any photo into clean notes, summaries, and instant answers—100% offline, private AI in your browser. Built for families by Michigan MindMend.
