# Michigan MindMend Inc. — Sponsor / Partner Brief

## One-Line Mission

Michigan MindMend Inc. builds privacy-first, offline-capable AI tools that support youth safety, family wellness, and community resilience without default data harvesting.

## Who We Serve

- Kids and families who need private support tools
- Rural and under-connected communities
- Caregivers, parents, and community organizations
- Nonprofits exploring safer AI adoption
- Developers building local-first AI systems

## Why This Matters

Many AI tools depend on cloud accounts, persistent logging, subscription access, and data collection. That model can fail families who need privacy, low-cost access, and tools that still work when connectivity is limited.

Michigan MindMend focuses on the opposite direction:

- local-first where possible
- privacy-first by design
- human oversight
- clear safety boundaries
- open-source learning and community trust

## Current Portfolio

### TrustLayer
LLM safety gateway prototype for privacy-aware chat-completion workflows.

### MindMend Guardian
Youth/family safety prototype exploring local, guardian-controlled support workflows.

### OpenClaw Empathy Anchor
Empathy and safety layer for journaling, check-ins, and sensitive support flows.

### HumaniCare AI / MindMend Vault
FastAPI starter scaffold for local-first health, mental-health, and community-resilience tools, with tests and CI.

### SnapNote
Photo-to-notes and summarization prototype for private everyday documents.

### Grok-1 / Dominus Ultra
AI systems learning projects focused on inference, RoPE, correctness, benchmarking, Triton, and PyTorch.

- Product validation and usability feedback

## Safety Boundaries

Michigan MindMend tools do not replace clinicians, therapists, crisis teams, emergency services, parents, guardians, or licensed professionals.

Any real-world healthcare, youth, or crisis-related deployment should include professional oversight, safety review, privacy review, and clear escalation paths.
